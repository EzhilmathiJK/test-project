Chicken Biriyani

"Chicken Biryani" is a classic Indian dish known for its aromatic flavors and rich taste. It is made with long-grain basmati rice, marinated chicken pieces, and a blend of spices. The dish is typically cooked in layers, with the marinated chicken cooked separately and then layered with partially cooked rice. Each layer is infused with spices, including cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves, along with fresh herbs like cilantro and mint. The layers are then steamed together, allowing the flavors to meld and the rice to absorb the fragrant spices. Chicken Biryani is often served with raita (a yogurt-based side dish) and is a favorite at festive occasions and celebrations.

Fried Rice

"Fried Rice" is a delightful Asian dish made with stir-fried rice, vegetables, and proteins, seasoned with soy sauce and aromatic spices. This dish offers a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, with each bite delivering a satisfying combination of savory and umami notes. The fluffy rice is complemented by the crunch of vegetables and the richness of eggs, creating a dish that is both comforting and flavorful. Fried Rice is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a main course or as a side dish, making it a favorite among those looking for a hearty and delicious meal.

₹ 150

BBQ Sausage Pizza

"BBQ Sausage Pizza" is a mouthwatering combination of smoky barbecue sauce, savory sausage, and melted cheese on a golden crust. This pizza is a flavor explosion, with each bite delivering a perfect balance of tangy, sweet, and spicy flavors. Topped with sliced red onions, bell peppers, and mushrooms, this pizza offers a delightful medley of textures and tastes. Perfect for pizza lovers seeking a bold and satisfying dining experience.

Mushroom Chilli Noodles

"Mushroom Chilli Noodles" is a savory and spicy dish that combines stir-fried noodles with mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, and a flavorful chili sauce. The dish is typically made by first cooking the noodles until they are al dente, then stir-frying them with sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions in a hot wok. The chili sauce, which can vary in spiciness depending on personal preference, is then added to the noodles and vegetables, giving the dish its signature spicy flavor. Mushroom Chilli Noodles are often garnished with fresh cilantro and green onions before serving, adding a fresh and vibrant touch to the dish.

₹ 180

Spicy Paneer Burger

"Spicy Paneer Noodles" is a tantalizing blend of soft paneer, stir-fried noodles, and crisp vegetables, all bathed in a spicy, aromatic sauce. This fusion dish combines the best of Indian and Chinese flavors, offering a symphony of tastes and textures in every bite. Perfectly marinated paneer adds a creamy richness, while the fiery sauce brings a bold kick. Garnished with fresh cilantro, this dish is a spicy delight for those craving a flavorful and satisfying meal.
